Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and the closest to the Sun.

Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is known for its thick atmosphere.

Earth

Earth is our home planet and the only known planet to support life.

Mars

Mars is known as the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance. It has polar ice caps, canyons, and evidence of past water flow on its surface.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system. It is a gas giant with a turbulent atmosphere and a distinct set of colorful bands and the Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Saturn is known for its prominent ring system made up of ice particles and debris. It is the second-largest planet and has dozens of moons.

Neptune

Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun and is an ice giant. It has a dynamic atmosphere with high-speed winds and the coldest temperatures in our solar system.
